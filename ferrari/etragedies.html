Wolfgang von Trips - Monza 1961

One of the worst tragedies in F1 history. Von Trips was battling for the title with teammate Phil Hill when his Ferrari, after making contact with Jim Clark, went airborne and crashed into the crowd. Von Trips and 14 spectators were killed.

Gilles Villeneuve - Zolder 1982

A darling of Enzo Ferrari and the fans, he died during qualifying for the Belgian Grand Prix after a collision with Jochen Massa. The accident occurred during a heated conflict with teammate Didier Pironi.

Luigi Musso - Reims-Gueux 1958

He died while chasing teammate Mike Hawthorne during the French Grand Prix.

Lorenzo Bandini - Monaco 1967

His car caught fire after hitting the barriers; Bandini suffered severe burns, from which he died in hospital three days later.
