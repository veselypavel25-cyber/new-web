Alberto Ascari: One of the greatest legends of motorsport, he won the entire championship twice. Unfortunately, he died in 1955 while testing a new formula.

Niki Lauda: Austrian Formula 1 racer, three-time world champion (1975, 1977, 1984) and successful aviation entrepreneur.

Fernando Alonso: Spanish Formula 1 racer, two-time world champion (2005, 2006) and one of the most experienced drivers in history, the first to surpass 400 Grand Prix starts.
