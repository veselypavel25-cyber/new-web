Enzo Ferrari
informations about Enzo:
- Italian motor racing driver, entrepreneur, and founder of the Ferrari
- born in 1898 in Modena(city in Italy)
- He worked as a test driver before joining Alfa Romeo in 1920 as a race car driver
- Founded Scuderia Ferrari in 1929 as a racing team for amateur drivers, it later became the official racing team for Alfa Romeo before producing its own cars.
- first official car was driven in 1947 (Ferrari 125 S), but year 1950 was remembered, because Ferrari became official brand in Formula 1 series.
