Enzo Ferrari
Informations about Enzo:
  • Italian motor racing driver, entrepreneur, and founder of Ferrari.
  • Born in 1898 in Modena, Italy.
  • Worked as a test driver before joining Alfa Romeo in 1920 as a race car driver.
  • Founded Scuderia Ferrari in 1929 as a racing team; it later became Alfa Romeo's official racing arm before producing its own cars.
  • The first official Ferrari car (125 S) was driven in 1947; Ferrari became a recognized Formula 1 constructor in the early 1950s.
