Best Drivers of Red Bull

Max Verstappen :
A four-time Formula One World Champion (2021–2024) renowned for his aggressive overtaking, exceptional speed, and record-breaking dominance with Red Bull Racing.

Sebastian Vettel :
A legendary German Formula 1 driver, widely known as a four-time consecutive World Champion (2010–2013) with Red Bull Racing.

Daniel Ricciardo :
Known for his aggressive driving style and his ability to overtake other drivers on the track. He has won several races and has been a consistent performer in Formula 1, earning a reputation as one of the most exciting drivers to watch.
